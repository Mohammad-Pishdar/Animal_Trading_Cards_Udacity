Persian Cheetah

The cheetah is the fastest land animal in the world.

  • Scientific Name: Acinonyx jubatus venaticus
  • Average Length: 112-135 cm (excluding the tail)
  • Average Lifespan: 10-12 years
  • Habitat: open lands, small plains and semi-desert areas

The Asiatic cheetah, also known as Iranian or Persian cheetah, is a Critically Endangered cheetah subspecies surviving today only in Iran. It once occurred from the Arabian Peninsula and the Near East to the Caspian region, Kyzylkum Desert, Pakistan and India, but has been extirpated there during the 20th century.
